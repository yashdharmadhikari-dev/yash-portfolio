Generating Textual Video Summaries using Modified Bi-Modal Transformer and Whisper Model

Published In: 2023 International Conference on Advanced Computing Technologies and Applications (ICACTA)

Date of Conference: 06-07 October 2023, Mumbai, India

Authors: Siddhesh Madkaikar, Vipul Belhekar, Yash Dharmadhikari, Amiya Kumar Tripathy

Abstract: Online video material availability has grown significantly over the past few years, making it challenging for viewers to consume and process information effectively. Video summaries written in text form have shown to be a helpful technique for solving this problem. The purpose of the model is to display a short textual summary of the video to the user before the user watches the complete video. This can be useful in various tasks such as video content moderation and assisting visually-impaired users.

Interactive 3D Bot with Generative AI

Description: Developed using cutting-edge technologies, this project integrates Unity 3D and Generative AI to create an innovative, interactive 3D bot. The bot is designed to serve as both a guide and an engaging companion, providing users with an entertaining and informative experience.

By leveraging Generative AI, the bot adapts to user inputs in real-time, offering dynamic responses and personalized interactions. The combination of immersive 3D environments powered by Unity and the intelligence of AI-driven responses creates a seamless, intuitive experience for users.

This project highlights the powerful convergence of AI and 3D technology, with potential applications across various industries. From interactive product tours and virtual assistants to educational platforms and entertainment services, the possibilities for this technology are vast, offering a new frontier in human-computer interaction.
